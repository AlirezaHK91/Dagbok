**This is a simple diary console application**
# MyDiary App

This repository contains a very simple diary application that allows users to create an account, log in, write, and manage their personal diary entries.

## Getting Started

To get started with the MyDiary app, follow these steps:

### Create an Account

1. **Sign Up:** Create an account by providing your first name, last name, username, and password.
2. **Login:** Use your registered username and password to log in to your account.

### Writing Entries

Once logged in, you can start writing your diary entries in your personal account. The app provides a secure space for you to jot down your thoughts and feelings.

### Change Password

Feel free to change your password anytime.

### Remove Account

If needed, you can remove your account and associated data permanently.
